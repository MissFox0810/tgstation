  • Xenomorph embryos now take a static five minutes (1 minute per 5 stages) to hatch, instead of taking any time between fifteen seconds, till the end of time itself to hatch. Aka it had a 3% chance and rolled every two seconds, now it uses a consistent timer.

  • You can now ride the banana glutton properly.
  • Bananas & peels have been resprited.
